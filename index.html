our mission

mission

At autobrush, we're dedicated to transforming the way we clean with our eco-friendly automatic dishbrush. Our mission is to offer innovative, sustainable solutions that simplify daily tasks while reducing environmental impact. Through thoughtful design and cutting-edge technology, we aim to empower individuals to maintain a clean home responsibly. Join us on our journey towards a greener, cleaner future—one dish at a time.

features

Soap dispenser

The autobrush automatically dispenses soap while the brush is active.
With a subscription, you don't need to worry about ever running out of soap.

Easy charging

The brush comes with rechargeable batteries and can be charged within the brush.

UV light

UV light emitter kills bacteria instantly.

Scraper

Our built-in scraper is specially designed to remove food that has dried in place.
