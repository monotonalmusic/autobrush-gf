mission

features

Soap dispenser

The autobrush effortlessly tackles tough stains, automatically dispensing soap for a seamless dishwashing experience. With a subscription, enjoy a constant supply of soap, eliminating worries about running out at inconvenient times. Elevate your kitchen routine with the autobrush—where innovation meets convenience.

Easy charging

The autobrush features rechargeable batteries for hassle-free power. Charge it effortlessly using solar power on a sunny windowsill or through the convenient USB-C port. With versatile charging options, the autobrush ensures it's always ready for action, seamlessly integrating into your daily routine.

UV light

The autobrush's UV light, which instantly kills bacteria and provides peace of mind, is seamlessly recharged along with the brush. This feature is particularly beneficial for dishes like cutting boards used for raw meat or fish. The UV light actively scans for bacteria during use and notifies you if any contaminants are detected, ensuring a hygienic dishwashing experience with added reassurance.

Scraper

The autobrush comes with a built-in scraper designed to easily remove dried-on food without causing any harm to your dishes. Streamline your dishwashing routine with this effective tool that complements the brush, ensuring a thorough clean for even the toughest spots.
